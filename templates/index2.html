Our Journey To Forever Family...

...continued!

Last time on "The Aldersons Are Adopting"...

It was 2013, and Nathan and Hannah decided to build their family with adoption. God called them to Eastern Europe, and then led them to four amazing kids in Poland. They were three, five, nine, and eleven at the time: three older brothers and their little sister. Nathan and Hannah flew to Poland, hung out with them for two months, and then finally brought them home less than a year after they filled out their first application paper.

Fast Forward to 2018...

In the fall of 2018, Nathan, Hannah, and their four kiddos (now eight, ten, fourteen, and sixteen) were marching along through the usual story lines: school, work, church, PTA meetings, sports (all the sports!). But God had a plot twist coming...

Nathan was in St. Louis for a work conference and Hannah was at church when our old social worker called us with the news that our kids' bio parents had had another baby. They wanted to know if we wanted to pursue another adoption.

A little while later, we told our kids about their sibling in a very fun way!

Where do you come in?

Last time we adopted, it was something we were able to plan, save, and fundraise for. This time, God's little plot twist came quite unexpectedly and we are not in the same financial situation. Unfortunately, we do not have $44,000 in savings to cover this adoption. We have spent nearly $10,000 from our savings already. We have received $12,000 in incredibly generous grants, and have applied for another $5000 or so. The whole adoption (including travel) will cost an estimated $44,000, which leaves us needing about $19,000 more in order to be fully funded and adopt debt free.

We know that when God called us to this adoption, he also provided everything we would need. We have already seen that in so many ways. So, if you would like to help us raise the funds we need to bring our little plot twist home, here's how you can give...
